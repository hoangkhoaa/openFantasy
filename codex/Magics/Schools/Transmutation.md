## Overview (概述)

Transmutation (炼金术) is the school of magic devoted to the fundamental transformation of matter and energy. Unlike conjuration (召唤术) that summons existing elements or evocation (塑能术) that generates magical effects, transmutation (炼金术) alters the intrinsic properties, form, or structure of targets. It encompasses everything from basic material changes to complex biological alterations and is regarded as perhaps the most technically demanding school of magic due to its precision requirements and comprehensive knowledge base.

炼金术是致力于物质和能量基本转化的魔法学院。与召唤现有元素的召唤术或产生魔法效果的塑能术不同，炼金术改变目标的内在属性、形式或结构。它涵盖从基本材料变化到复杂生物改造的一切，并且由于其精确的要求和全面的知识基础，被认为是技术上要求最高的魔法学院。
