# **The School of Transmutation (変成術学派)**

> *"To transmute is to understand the deepest nature of matter and energy—to see not what is, but what could be. All substance is but patterns waiting to be rearranged."*

## Overview (概要)

Transmutation (変成術) is the school of magic devoted to the fundamental transformation of matter and energy. (物質とエネルギーの根本的な変換に特化した魔法の学派です。) Unlike conjuration that summons existing elements or evocation that generates magical effects, transmutation (変成術) alters the intrinsic properties, form, or structure of targets. (既存の元素を召喚する召喚術や、魔法効果を生み出す喚起術とは異なり、変成術は対象の本質的な特性、形状、または構造を変化させます。) It encompasses everything from basic material changes to complex biological alterations and is regarded as perhaps the most technically demanding school of magic due to its precision requirements and comprehensive knowledge base. (基本的な物質の変化から複雑な生物学的変化までを網羅しており、その精度要件と包括的な知識ベースから、おそらく最も技術的に要求の厳しい魔法の学派と見なされています。)
