# **Mana Purity (魔力纯度)**: The Refinement of Magical Energy (魔法能量的提纯)

## Overview (概述)

Mana Purity (魔力纯度) refers to the degree of refinement and control present in magical energy (魔法能量) used for spellcasting (施法). It represents how thoroughly a caster (施法者) has processed raw magical energy (原始魔法能量) into a form that is stable, precise, and aligned with their intentions.

Mana Purity (魔力纯度) is a core metric in determining spell effectiveness, with higher purity typically yielding more controlled and predictable results.

魔力纯度是决定法术效力的核心指标，通常较高的纯度会产生更可控和可预测的结果。

## The Two-Phase Conversion Process (两阶段转化过程)

Magical energy (魔法能量) undergoes two distinct phases of processing before being used in spellcasting (施法):

魔法能量在使用于施法之前，会经历两个不同的处理阶段：

### Phase 1: Wild Mana (狂野魔力) to Base Mana (基础魔力) Conversion (转化)
- **Input (输入)**: Raw Wild Mana (原始狂野魔力) from the environment (环境)
- **Process (过程)**: Biological absorption (生物吸收) and initial stabilization (初始稳定) within a creature's body (生物体内)
- **Output (输出)**: Base Mana (基础魔力) stored within the caster's (施法者) internal reserves (体内储备)
- **Key Metric (关键指标)**: Conversion Effectiveness (转化效率) (quantity of usable energy produced (产生的可用能量数量))
- **Primary Function (主要功能)**: Transforms chaotic environmental energy (混乱的环境能量) into stable, internally stored energy (稳定的，体内储存的能量)

> **Important (重要)**: This first phase determines how efficiently a being converts Wild Mana (狂野魔力) into usable Base Mana (可用基础魔力), directly affecting their available magical reserves and regeneration rate (魔法储备和回复速度). Most beings perform this conversion unconsciously as a natural biological function.

> **重要**: 第一阶段决定了一个生物将狂野魔力转化为可用基础魔力的效率，直接影响他们可用的魔法储备和回复速度。大多数生物无意识地执行这种转化，作为一种自然的生物功能。

### Phase 2: Base Mana (基础魔力) to Elemental Mana (元素魔力) Conversion (转化)
- **Input (输入)**: Base Mana (基础魔力) from the caster's (施法者) internal reserves (体内储备)
- **Process (过程)**: Intentional refinement (有意提纯) and specialization (专业化) through conscious magical technique (有意识的魔法技巧)
- **Output (输出)**: Elemental Mana (元素魔力) + residual Wild Mana (残留狂野魔力)
- **Key Metric (关键指标)**: Mana Purity (魔力纯度) (quality of the resulting magical energy (产生的魔法能量的质量))
- **Primary Function (主要功能)**: Transforms neutral Base Mana (中性基础魔力) into specialized forms (特殊形式) suitable for spellcasting (施法)

> **Critical Understanding (关键理解)**: Purity percentage (纯度百分比) is calculated during this second phase as the ratio of Refined Elemental Mana (精炼元素魔力) to total mana (总魔力) (Refined Elemental Mana (精炼元素魔力) + Residual Wild Mana (残留狂野魔力)).

> **关键理解**: 纯度百分比是在第二阶段计算的，作为精炼元素魔力与总魔力（精炼元素魔力 + 残留狂野魔力）的比率。

## Measuring Purity (纯度的测量)

### Mathematical Expression (数学表达式)
Purity calculations follow the formula:
纯度计算遵循以下公式：
```
Purity Percentage = (Refined Elemental Mana) / (Refined Elemental Mana + Residual Wild Mana) × 100%
纯度百分比 = (精炼元素魔力) / (精炼元素魔力 + 残留狂野魔力) × 100%
```

For example, if a spell contains 30 units of refined Fire Mana (火焰魔力) and 70 units of residual Wild Mana (残留狂野魔力), its purity would be 30/(30+70) × 100% = 30% purity.

例如，如果一个法术包含 30 个单位的精炼火焰魔力 和 70 个单位的残留狂野魔力，那么它的纯度将为 30/(30+70) × 100% = 30% 纯度。

- **Pattern Conflicts (模式冲突)**: Contradictory elements in a spell design (法术设计中矛盾的元素) can create "leaks" (会产生“泄漏”)
