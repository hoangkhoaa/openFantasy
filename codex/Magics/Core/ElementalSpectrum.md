## Spectrum Overview (스펙트럼 개요)

The Elemental Mana Spectrum (원소 마나 스펙트럼) represents the complete range of specialized mana types that can be derived from neutral [Base Mana](/codex/Magics/Core/BaseMana.md) (기본 마나) through the process of [Mana Specialization](/codex/Magics/Core/ManaSpecialization.md) (마나 특화). This spectrum follows specific patterns and relationships that determine how elemental energies interact, combine, and oppose one another. Understanding these relationships is essential for advanced spellcasting, particularly when working with multiple elements simultaneously.

### Visualization of the Spectrum (스펙트럼 시각화)

The Elemental Spectrum (원소 스펙트럼) can be visualized in several ways:

1. **The Chromatic Circle (색채 원)**: A circular arrangement showing elemental transitions and oppositions (원소의 변화와 대립을 보여주는 원형 배열)
2. **The Harmonic Scale (조화 척도)**: A linear representation emphasizing resonance relationships (공명 관계를 강조하는 선형 표현)
3. **The Three-Dimensional Matrix (3차원 행렬)**: A spatial model revealing complex interaction potentials (복잡한 상호 작용 가능성을 보여주는 공간 모델)
4. **The Wavelength Continuum (파장 연속체)**: A representation of elements as varying frequencies of Base Mana (기본 마나의 다양한 주파수로서 원소를 표현)

Each visualization offers different insights into elemental relationships.

### Elemental Affinities and Training (원소 친화력 및 훈련)

Most casters have natural affinities for certain elements and resistance to others. Training typically progresses in this sequence:

1. **Affinity Element (친화 원소)**: Beginning with one's strongest natural elemental connection (가장 강한 자연적 원소 연결로 시작)
2. **Adjacent Elements (인접 원소)**: Moving to elements with harmonic compatibility (조화로운 호환성을 가진 원소로 이동)
3. **Complementary Elements (상호 보완적 원소)**: Learning elements that enhance one's affinity (자신의 친화력을 향상시키는 원소 학습)

5. **Resistant Elements (저항 원소)**: Gradually developing skill with difficult elements (어려운 원소로 점차 기술 개발)
6. **Opposed Elements (대립 원소)**: Finally attempting one's natural opposition (마지막으로 자연스러운 대립 시도)
