## Overview (概述)

Mana Specialization (法力专精) refers to the conscious, technical process by which casters transform neutral [Base Mana](/codex/Magics/Core/BaseMana.md) (基础法力) into specific Elemental Mana (元素法力) types suitable for spellcasting. This transformation represents Phase 2 of the magical energy conversion continuum and is the defining skill that separates trained spellcasters from those with mere magical potential. Unlike the largely biological process of Wild Mana (狂野法力) conversion (Phase 1), specialization is a learned skill requiring precision, focus, and understanding of elemental resonances.

法力专精指的是施法者有意识、有技巧地将无属性的 [Base Mana (基础法力)](/codex/Magics/Core/BaseMana.md) 转化为适合施法的特定 Elemental Mana (元素法力) 类型。这种转化代表了魔法能量转换过程的第二阶段，也是区分训练有素的施法者和仅有魔法潜力之人的关键技能。与主要依靠生物过程的 Wild Mana (狂野法力) 转化（第一阶段）不同，专精是一项需要精准、专注和理解元素共振的后天技能。
