## Properties of Hydromana (ハイドロマナの特性)

Hydromana (ハイドロマナ), the elemental mana of water (水の元素マナ), is characterized by:

- **Appearance (外観)**: Flowing blues and cyans with silver highlights; translucent and reflective (流れるような青とシアン、銀色のハイライト。半透明で反射性)
- **Sensation (感覚)**: Feels cool, soothing, and fluid when channeled; creates a gentle pressure (チャネリングすると、冷たく、心地よく、流動的に感じる。穏やかな圧力を生み出す)
- **Resonance (共鳴)**: Medium-frequency, flowing vibration patterns with gentle oscillations (中周波、穏やかな振動を伴う流れるような振動パターン)
- **Purification Challenge (浄化の課題)**: Absorbs impurities easily, requiring multiple filtration cycles; tends to carry and hold contamination (不純物を吸収しやすく、複数回のろ過サイクルが必要。汚染物質を運び保持する傾向がある)
- **Opposite Element (反対の元素)**: Fire (creates steam when forced together, ultimately extinguishes fire) (火（強制的に合わせると蒸気を発生させ、最終的には火を消す）)
- **Complementary Elements (相補的な元素)**: Air (creates mist/fog), Death (creates ice) (空気（霧/靄を生成）、死（氷を生成）)
- **Musical Analogy (音楽的な類似性)**: Cello and flowing string instruments; sustained notes that rise and fall like waves (チェロと流れるような弦楽器。波のように昇り降りする持続音)

## Manipulation Techniques (操作技術)

### Basic Techniques (基本技術)

#### Aqueous Projection (水性投影)
The foundational technique of directing and projecting water (水を誘導し投影するための基礎技術):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Novice (初心者)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Basic (15-25%) (基本（15-25%）)
- **Effect (効果)**: Manipulates existing water sources or draws moisture from the air (既存の水源を操作するか、空気から水分を引き出す)
- **Training Focus (トレーニングの焦点)**: Flow control and directional precision (流れの制御と方向の精度)
- **Applications (応用)**: Moving water between containers, creating small currents, gathering scattered droplets (容器間の水の移動、小さな流れの作成、散在する水滴の収集)

#### State Transition (状態遷移)
Changing water between its three primary states (水の3つの主要な状態を変化させる):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Novice to Intermediate (初心者から中級者)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Basic to Refined (15-40%) (基本から洗練（15-40%）)
- **Effect (効果)**: Transforms water between liquid, solid (ice), and gaseous (vapor) states (水を液体、固体（氷）、気体（蒸気）の状態に変換する)
- **Training Focus (トレーニングの焦点)**: Temperature control and molecular arrangement (温度制御と分子配列)
- **Applications (応用)**: Creating ice shapes, generating mist, condensing water from humid air (氷の形状の作成、霧の生成、湿った空気からの水の凝縮)

#### Pressure Manipulation (圧力操作)
Controlling the pressure and force of water (水の圧力と力を制御する):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Intermediate (中級者)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Refined (30-45%) (洗練（30-45%）)
- **Effect (効果)**: Increases or decreases water pressure and directional force (水圧と方向力を増減させる)
- **Training Focus (トレーニングの焦点)**: Force vectors and pressure gradients (力ベクトルと圧力勾配)
- **Applications (応用)**: Water jets, underwater breathing spheres, pressure reduction for deep diving (ウォータージェット、水中呼吸球、深海潜水のための圧力低減)

### Advanced Techniques (高度な技術)

#### Hydromantic Shaping (水ロマンチックな整形)
Creating and maintaining complex water structures (複雑な水の構造を作成し維持する):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Advanced (上級者)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Refined to Perfect (40-75%) (洗練から完璧（40-75%）)
- **Effect (効果)**: Forms water into complex, self-sustaining shapes that maintain their form (形状を維持する複雑で自立可能な形状に水を成形する)
- **Training Focus (トレーニングの焦点)**: Surface tension control and internal circulation (表面張力制御と内部循環)
- **Applications (応用)**: Water constructs, liquid barriers, floating water platforms (水の構造物、液体のバリア、浮遊する水のプラットフォーム)

#### Phased Hydromancy (段階的水占い)
Simultaneous control of multiple water states (複数の水の状態を同時に制御する):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Expert (専門家)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Perfect (65-85%) (完璧（65-85%）)
- **Effect (効果)**: Manipulates liquid, solid, and vapor forms of water simultaneously (水、固体、蒸気の形態を同時に操作する)
- **Training Focus (トレーニングの焦点)**: Multiple-state awareness and transition boundaries (多状態の認識と遷移境界)
- **Applications (応用)**: Mixed-state attacks, environmental control, complex illusions (混合状態攻撃、環境制御、複雑な幻想)

#### Essence Extraction (エッセンス抽出)
Drawing water from unlikely sources (ありえない水源から水を引き出す):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Master (達人)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Perfect (70-90%) (完璧（70-90%）)
- **Effect (効果)**: Extracts water molecules from organic matter, minerals, or elemental compounds (有機物、鉱物、または元素化合物から水分子を抽出する)
- **Training Focus (トレーニングの焦点)**: Molecular separation and attraction (分子分離と引力)
- **Applications (応用)**: Survival in arid environments, purification, weakening waterlogged structures (乾燥した環境での生存、浄化、水浸しになった構造の弱体化)

## Risks and Safeguards (リスクと安全対策)

### Common Risks (一般的なリスク)

- **Hydration Imbalance (水分不均衡)**: Drawing too much moisture from one's own body during casting (詠唱中に自分の体から過剰な水分を引き出す)
- **Thermal Shock (熱ショック)**: Rapid transitions between water states causing system strain (水の状態間の急速な移行がシステムの負担を引き起こす)
- **Pressure Trauma (圧力外傷)**: Improper pressure manipulation damaging ears, sinuses, or lungs (不適切な圧力操作が耳、副鼻腔、または肺を損傷する)
- **Drowning Resonance (溺水共鳴)**: Creating breathable water with imperfect patterns, causing respiratory issues (不完全なパターンで呼吸可能な水を作り、呼吸器系の問題を引き起こす)
- **Ice Crystallization (氷の結晶化)**: Improper freezing techniques causing tissue damage (不適切な凍結技術が組織損傷を引き起こす)

### Miscast Effects (詠唱ミス効果)

| **Moderate (中等度)** | Impaired temperature regulation, mild hypothermia, internal moisture condensation (体温調節の障害、軽度の低体温、内部の水分凝縮) | Days (日) |

| **Critical (致命的)** | Cellular crystallization, major osmotic shock, total hydration system failure (細胞結晶化、重度の浸透圧ショック、完全な水分補給システム障害) | Months (月) |

- **Hydration Discipline (水分補給の規律)**: Maintaining proper personal hydration before and after spellcasting (詠唱の前後で適切な個人的な水分補給を維持する)
- **Thermal Gradient Training (温度勾配トレーニング)**: Practicing gradual temperature transitions (段階的な温度遷移を練習する)

- **Seasonal Awareness (季節の認識)**: Adjusting techniques based on environmental conditions (環境条件に基づいて技術を調整する)
