## Properties of Umbramana (暗影能量的属性)

Umbramana (暗影能量), the elemental mana of shadow (阴影的元素能量), is characterized by:

- **Appearance (外观)**: Deep purples, blues, and blacks; absorptive, flowing patterns that seem to draw in light (深紫色、蓝色和黑色；吸收性的、流动性的图案，似乎能吸收光线)
- **Sensation (感觉)**: Feels cool, calming, and subtly invigorating when channeled; creates a sense of depth and concealment (引导时感觉凉爽、平静，并略微令人精神焕发；创造一种深度和隐蔽感)
- **Resonance (共鸣)**: Low to medium-frequency, complex vibration patterns with harmonic depth (低到中频，具有谐波深度的复杂振动模式)
- **Purification Challenge (净化挑战)**: Tends to absorb and hide impurities rather than release them; requires persistent refinement (倾向于吸收和隐藏杂质而不是释放它们；需要持续的提炼)
- **Opposite Element (相反元素)**: Light (creates twilight when forced together, ultimately consumed by light) (光（强制结合时产生暮光，最终被光吞噬）)
- **Complementary Elements (互补元素)**: Death (creates void), Fire (creates smoke) (死亡（创造虚空），火焰（创造烟雾）)
- **Musical Analogy (音乐类比)**: Minor key melodies, particularly string instruments; complex, layered harmonies (小调旋律，尤其是弦乐器；复杂的分层和声)

## Manipulation Techniques (操纵技巧)

### Basic Techniques (基本技巧)

#### Shadow Summoning (阴影召唤)
The foundational technique of drawing forth and concentrating shadow: (汲取和集中阴影的基础技巧)
- **Difficulty (难度)**: Novice (新手)
- **Purity Requirement (纯度要求)**: Basic (15-25%) (基础 (15-25%))
- **Effect (效果)**: Gathers ambient darkness or creates shadow where minimal light exists (聚集环境中的黑暗或在光线极少的地方创造阴影)
- **Training Focus (训练重点)**: Darkness concentration and boundary definition (黑暗集中和边界定义)
- **Applications (应用)**: Creating areas of concealment, darkening rooms, enhancing existing shadows (创造隐蔽区域，使房间变暗，增强现有阴影)

#### Umbral Shaping (暗影塑形)
Molding and manipulating existing shadow: (塑造和操纵现有阴影)
- **Difficulty (难度)**: Novice to Intermediate (新手到中级)
- **Purity Requirement (纯度要求)**: Basic to Refined (20-40%) (基础到精炼 (20-40%))
- **Effect (效果)**: Forms shadow into specific shapes and patterns (将阴影塑造成特定的形状和图案)
- **Training Focus (训练重点)**: Shadow density and form maintenance (阴影密度和形态维持)
- **Applications (应用)**: Simple disguises, intimidation effects, symbolic communication (简单的伪装，恐吓效果，象征性交流)

#### Perception Dimming (感知削弱)
Subtly affecting how others perceive the caster: (微妙地影响他人对施法者的感知)
- **Difficulty (难度)**: Intermediate (中级)
- **Purity Requirement (纯度要求)**: Refined (30-50%) (精炼 (30-50%))
- **Effect (效果)**: Reduces observers' awareness of the caster's presence (减少观察者对施法者存在的意识)
- **Training Focus (训练重点)**: Attention deflection and sensory dampening (注意力转移和感官抑制)
- **Applications (应用)**: Stealth enhancement, social camouflage, avoiding unwanted attention (隐身增强，社交伪装，避免不必要的关注)

- **Applications (应用)**: Concealment of objects, temporary storage, escape routes (隐藏物体，临时存储，逃生路线)

Using shadow as a medium for transportation: (使用阴影作为交通媒介)
