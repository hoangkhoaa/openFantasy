## Properties of Terramana (テラマナの特性)

Terramana (テラマナ), the elemental mana of earth (地の元素マナ), is characterized by:

- **Appearance (外観)**: Deep browns, greens, and amber; crystalline structure with geometric patterns (深い茶色、緑、琥珀色。幾何学的な模様を持つ結晶構造)
- **Sensation (感覚)**: Feels heavy, grounding, and solid when channeled; creates a sense of weight and stability (チャネリングすると、重く、大地に根ざし、しっかりとした感じ。重さと安定感を生み出す)
- **Resonance (共鳴)**: Low-frequency, stable vibration patterns with strong harmonic undertones (低周波で安定した振動パターン、強いハーモニック・アンダートーンを持つ)
- **Purification Challenge (浄化の難易度)**: Slow to purify but maintains purity once achieved; tends to accumulate sedimentary impurities (浄化には時間がかかるが、一度達成すると純度を維持する。堆積性の不純物を蓄積しやすい)
- **Opposite Element (反対の元素)**: Air (creates dust when forced together, ultimately eroded by air) (空気 (無理に混ぜると塵を作り、最終的には空気によって浸食される))
- **Complementary Elements (相補的な元素)**: Fire (creates magma/lava), Life (creates growth and fertility) (火 (マグマ/溶岩を作る), 生命 (成長と豊穣をもたらす))
- **Musical Analogy (音楽的類似性)**: Bass instruments, particularly double bass; foundation notes that provide structure (低音楽器、特にコントラバス。構造を提供する基礎音)

## Manipulation Techniques (操作技術)

### Basic Techniques (基本的な技術)

#### Terrakinesis (テラキネシス)
The foundational technique of moving and directing earth and stone (土や石を動かし、操る基礎的な技術):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Novice (初心者)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Basic (15-30%) (基本 (15-30%))
- **Effect (効果)**: Shifts, lifts, or compresses earth, sand, or loose stone (土、砂、またはゆるい石を移動、持ち上げ、圧縮する)
- **Training Focus (訓練の焦点)**: Force direction and mass distribution (力の方向と質量分布)
- **Applications (応用)**: Creating mounds, digging trenches, stabilizing unstable ground (塚を作る、溝を掘る、不安定な地面を安定させる)

#### Material Densification (物質密度化)
Increasing or decreasing the density and hardness of earth materials (土の物質の密度と硬度を増減させる):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Novice to Intermediate (初心者から中級者)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Basic to Refined (20-45%) (基本から洗練 (20-45%))
- **Effect (効果)**: Changes the physical properties of earth without changing its form (形状を変えずに土の物理的特性を変える)
- **Training Focus (訓練の焦点)**: Molecular compression and structural integrity (分子圧縮と構造的完全性)
- **Applications (応用)**: Hardening mud into clay or stone, softening rock, creating paths through difficult terrain (泥を粘土や石に変える、岩を柔らかくする、困難な地形に道を作る)

#### Seismic Sensing (地震感知)
Detecting vibrations and structures through earth (土を通して振動や構造を感知する):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Intermediate (中級者)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Refined (35-50%) (洗練 (35-50%))
- **Effect (効果)**: Extends awareness through connected earth materials (繋がっている土の物質を通して意識を拡大する)
- **Training Focus (訓練の焦点)**: Vibrational interpretation and filtering (振動の解釈とフィルタリング)
- **Applications (応用)**: Finding caverns, detecting movement, locating water or mineral deposits (洞窟を見つける、動きを検知する、水や鉱物の堆積場所を特定する)

### Advanced Techniques (高度な技術)

#### Crystalline Formation (結晶形成)
Creating and manipulating mineral structures (鉱物構造を作り、操作する):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Advanced (上級者)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Refined to Perfect (45-70%) (洗練から完璧 (45-70%))
- **Effect (効果)**: Forms crystalline structures with specific properties and geometric patterns (特定の特性と幾何学的なパターンを持つ結晶構造を形成する)
- **Training Focus (訓練の焦点)**: Molecular arrangement and growth control (分子配列と成長制御)
- **Applications (応用)**: Creating gemstones, reinforcing structures, developing crystalline tools (宝石を作る、構造を強化する、結晶ツールを開発する)

#### Terra Transmutation (土壌変成)
Changing one earth material into another (ある土の物質を別のものに変える):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Expert (専門家)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Perfect (65-85%) (完璧 (65-85%))
- **Effect (効果)**: Transforms the elemental composition of earth materials (土の物質の元素組成を変える)
- **Training Focus (訓練の焦点)**: Molecular deconstruction and reassembly (分子の分解と再構築)
- **Applications (応用)**: Creating precious metals, purifying ores, transforming sand to glass (貴金属を作る、鉱石を精製する、砂をガラスに変える)

#### Tectonic Manipulation (地殻操作)
Influencing the movement of large earth masses (大規模な土塊の動きに影響を与える):
- **Difficulty (難易度)**: Master (達人)
- **Purity Requirement (純度要件)**: Perfect (75-95%) (完璧 (75-95%))
- **Effect (効果)**: Creates controlled movement in bedrock and underground structures (岩盤と地下構造において制御された動きを生み出す)
- **Training Focus (訓練の焦点)**: Force multiplication and geological understanding (力の増幅と地質学的理解)
- **Applications (応用)**: Creating passages through mountains, stabilizing earthquake-prone regions, raising small landforms (山に道を作る、地震の多い地域を安定させる、小さな地形を隆起させる)

## Affinity Development (親和性の発達)

Earth affinity (土の親和性) progresses through several distinctive stages:

### Grounded Connection (根源的な繋がり)
The foundational relationship with earth that most beginners possess (ほとんどの初心者が持つ、土との基礎的な関係):
- **Manifestation (顕現)**: Intuitive sense of stability, minor influence over soil and sand (安定感に対する直感的な感覚、土壌と砂に対するわずかな影響)
- **Physical Signs (身体的兆候)**: Slightly roughened skin, strong sense of direction underground (わずかにざらついた肌、地下での強い方向感覚)
- **Enhancement Method (強化方法)**: Regular meditation while in direct contact with bare earth (裸の土と直接接触しながら定期的に瞑想する)

### Material Mastery (物質の熟達)
The intermediate stage where earth responds more readily to the mage's will (土が魔法使いの意思により容易に反応する中級段階):
- **Manifestation (顕現)**: Stone and earth move with less effort, sensing abilities expand (石や土が少ない労力で動き、感知能力が拡大する)
- **Physical Signs (身体的兆候)**: Nails and hair take on mineral-like properties, skin develops subtle crystal patterns (爪や髪が鉱物のような性質を帯び、皮膚に微妙な結晶パターンが現れる)
- **Enhancement Method (強化方法)**: Handling and studying different types of stone, soil, and crystal (さまざまな種類の石、土壌、クリスタルを扱い、研究する)

### Bedrock Harmony (岩盤の調和)
Advanced practitioners achieve a deep resonance with earth's structural properties (上級者は土の構造的特性との深い共鳴を達成する):
- **Manifestation (顕現)**: Earth seems to respond intuitively, minerals form naturally around the caster (土が直感的に反応するように見え、鉱物が詠唱者の周りに自然に形成される)

## Risks and Safeguards (リスクと安全対策)

- **Immobility (不動性)**: Becoming too attuned to earth's stillness, resulting in slowed movement (土の静止に同調しすぎると、動きが鈍くなる)
- **Crystal Growth (結晶成長)**: Unintended crystallization in the caster's body tissues (詠唱者の体内組織における意図しない結晶化)
- **Mass Overload (質量過負荷)**: Attempting to manipulate earth masses beyond one's strength (自分の力を超えて土塊を操作しようとする)
- **Burial Potential (埋葬の可能性)**: Loss of control leading to self-burial or cave-ins (制御を失うと、自らの埋葬または陥没につながる)
- **Molecular Disruption (分子破壊)**: Improper transmutation affecting the caster's own body composition (不適切な変成が詠唱者自身の体の組成に影響を与える)
