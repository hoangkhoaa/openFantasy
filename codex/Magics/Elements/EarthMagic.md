## Properties of Terramana (地源玛那的属性)

Terramana (地源玛那), the elemental mana of earth (土系元素玛那), is characterized by:

- **Appearance** (外观): Deep browns, greens, and amber (深棕色、绿色和琥珀色); crystalline structure with geometric patterns (具有几何图案的晶体结构)
- **Sensation** (感觉): Feels heavy, grounding, and solid when channeled (引导时感觉沉重、踏实和坚固); creates a sense of weight and stability (产生重量和稳定的感觉)
- **Resonance** (共鸣): Low-frequency, stable vibration patterns with strong harmonic undertones (低频、稳定的振动模式，带有强烈的谐波)
- **Purification Challenge** (净化挑战): Slow to purify but maintains purity once achieved (净化缓慢，但一旦净化完成即可保持纯净); tends to accumulate sedimentary impurities (易于积累沉积杂质)
- **Opposite Element** (相克元素): Air (空气) (creates dust when forced together, ultimately eroded by air (强制混合时产生灰尘，最终会被空气侵蚀))
- **Complementary Elements** (互补元素): Fire (火焰) (creates magma/lava (产生岩浆/熔岩)), Life (生命) (creates growth and fertility (创造生长和肥沃))
- **Musical Analogy** (音乐类比): Bass instruments, particularly double bass (低音乐器，尤其是低音提琴); foundation notes that provide structure (提供结构的基础音符)

## Manipulation Techniques (操控技巧)

### Basic Techniques (基础技巧)

#### Terrakinesis
The foundational technique of moving and directing earth and stone (移动和控制土石的基础技巧):
- **Difficulty** (难度): Novice (新手)
- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Basic (基础) (15-30%)
- **Effect** (效果): Shifts, lifts, or compresses earth, sand, or loose stone (移动、抬升或压缩土壤、沙子或松散的石头)
- **Training Focus** (训练重点): Force direction and mass distribution (力的方向和质量分布)
- **Applications** (应用): Creating mounds (制造土堆), digging trenches (挖掘壕沟), stabilizing unstable ground (稳定不稳定的地面)

#### Material Densification (材料致密化)
Increasing or decreasing the density and hardness of earth materials (增加或减少土系材料的密度和硬度):
- **Difficulty** (难度): Novice to Intermediate (新手到中级)
- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Basic to Refined (基础到精炼) (20-45%)
- **Effect** (效果): Changes the physical properties of earth without changing its form (改变土壤的物理性质而不改变其形态)
- **Training Focus** (训练重点): Molecular compression and structural integrity (分子压缩和结构完整性)
- **Applications** (应用): Hardening mud into clay or stone (将泥土硬化为粘土或石头), softening rock (软化岩石), creating paths through difficult terrain (在困难地形中开辟道路)

#### Seismic Sensing (地震感知)
Detecting vibrations and structures through earth (通过地球探测振动和结构):
- **Difficulty** (难度): Intermediate (中级)
- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Refined (精炼) (35-50%)
- **Effect** (效果): Extends awareness through connected earth materials (通过连接的土系材料扩展感知)
- **Training Focus** (训练重点): Vibrational interpretation and filtering (振动解释和过滤)
- **Applications** (应用): Finding caverns (寻找洞穴), detecting movement (探测移动), locating water or mineral deposits (定位水源或矿藏)

### Advanced Techniques (高级技巧)

#### Crystalline Formation (晶体形成)
Creating and manipulating mineral structures (创造和操控矿物结构):
- **Difficulty** (难度): Advanced (高级)
- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Refined to Perfect (精炼到完美) (45-70%)
- **Effect** (效果): Forms crystalline structures with specific properties and geometric patterns (形成具有特定属性和几何图案的晶体结构)
- **Training Focus** (训练重点): Molecular arrangement and growth control (分子排列和生长控制)
- **Applications** (应用): Creating gemstones (制造宝石), reinforcing structures (加固结构), developing crystalline tools (开发晶体工具)

#### Terra Transmutation (大地转化)
Changing one earth material into another (将一种土系材料转化为另一种):
- **Difficulty** (难度): Expert (专家)
- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Perfect (完美) (65-85%)
- **Effect** (效果): Transforms the elemental composition of earth materials (改变土系材料的元素组成)
- **Training Focus** (训练重点): Molecular deconstruction and reassembly (分子解构和重组)
- **Applications** (应用): Creating precious metals (制造贵金属), purifying ores (提纯矿石), transforming sand to glass (将沙子转化为玻璃)

#### Tectonic Manipulation (地质操控)
Influencing the movement of large earth masses (影响大型土体的运动):
- **Difficulty** (难度): Master (大师)
- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Perfect (完美) (75-95%)
- **Effect** (效果): Creates controlled movement in bedrock and underground structures (在基岩和地下结构中创造可控的运动)
- **Training Focus** (训练重点): Force multiplication and geological understanding (力量倍增和地质理解)
- **Applications** (应用): Creating passages through mountains (在山中开辟通道), stabilizing earthquake-prone regions (稳定地震多发地区), raising small landforms (抬升小型地形)

- **Crystal Growth** (晶体生长): Unintended crystallization in the caster's body tissues (施法者身体组织中意外的结晶)
- **Mass Overload** (质量超载): Attempting to manipulate earth masses beyond one's strength (试图操纵超出自身力量的土体)
- **Burial Potential** (埋葬风险): Loss of control leading to self-burial or cave-ins (失控导致自我埋葬或塌方)
