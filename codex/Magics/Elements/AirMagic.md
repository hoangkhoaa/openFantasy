## Properties of Aeromana (气灵力的属性)

Aeromana (气灵力), the elemental mana of air (空气的元素魔法), is characterized by:

- **Appearance** (外观): Transparent whites and pale blues; swirling, ethereal patterns that constantly shift (透明的白色和浅蓝色；不断变化的漩涡状、空灵的图案)
- **Sensation** (感觉): Feels light, refreshing, and dynamic when channeled; creates a sense of weightlessness (引导时感觉轻盈、清爽和充满活力；产生失重感)
- **Resonance** (共鸣): High to medium-frequency, constantly changing vibration patterns with harmonic overtones (高中频，不断变化的振动模式，带有谐波泛音)
- **Purification Challenge** (净化挑战): Difficult to contain during purification, but accepts purification easily; tends to disperse rapidly (净化过程中难以控制，但容易接受净化；倾向于迅速扩散)
- **Opposite Element** (对立元素): Earth (土) (creates dust when forced together, ultimately erodes earth (当强制结合时产生灰尘，最终侵蚀土地))
- **Complementary Elements** (互补元素): Water (水) (creates mist/fog (产生薄雾/雾)), Light (光) (creates radiance (产生光辉))
- **Musical Analogy** (音乐类比): Flute and wind instruments (长笛和管乐器); notes that rise and fall, creating airy melodies (音符起伏，创造空灵的旋律)

## Manipulation Techniques (操控技巧)

### Basic Techniques (基础技巧)

#### Air Current Control (气流控制)
The foundational technique of directing air movement (引导空气运动的基础技巧):
- **Difficulty** (难度): Novice (新手)
- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Basic (基础) (15-25%)
- **Effect** (效果): Creates, redirects, or calms air flow (创造、重定向或平息气流)
- **Training Focus** (训练重点): Pressure differential creation and flow dynamics (压力差创建和流动动力学)
- **Applications** (应用): Creating breezes, deflecting objects, cooling areas, enhancing combustion (创造微风、偏转物体、冷却区域、增强燃烧)

#### Pressure Manipulation (压力操控)
Altering air pressure in localized areas (改变局部区域的气压):
- **Difficulty** (难度): Novice (新手) to Intermediate (中级)
- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Basic (基础) to Refined (精炼) (20-40%)
- **Effect** (效果): Creates pockets of high or low pressure (创造高压或低压区)
- **Training Focus** (训练重点): Compression dynamics and boundary maintenance (压缩动力学和边界维持)
- **Applications** (应用): Sound amplification or dampening, creating cushioning effects, weather influence (声音放大或减弱、创造缓冲效果、影响天气)

#### Breath Enhancement (呼吸增强)
Controlling breath and respiratory efficiency (控制呼吸和呼吸效率):
- **Difficulty** (难度): Intermediate (中级)
- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Refined (精炼) (30-45%)
- **Effect** (效果): Improves respiratory function and vocal projection (改善呼吸功能和声音投射)
- **Training Focus** (训练重点): Fine control of air in biological systems (精细控制生物系统中的空气)
- **Applications** (应用): Extended breath holding, voice projection, filtering harmful substances (延长屏气时间、声音投射、过滤有害物质)

### Advanced Techniques (高级技巧)

#### Aerial Shaping (空中塑形)

- **Difficulty** (难度): Advanced (高级)

- **Applications** (应用): Air barriers, floating platforms, sound-channeling tubes, stable vortices (空气屏障、漂浮平台、声音通道管、稳定涡流)

Interpreting and influencing natural air currents (解释和影响自然气流):
- **Difficulty** (难度): Expert (专家)
- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Perfect (完美) (60-80%)

- **Applications** (应用): Weather prediction, storm calming, wind direction change, cloud formation (天气预测、风暴平息、风向改变、云形成)

- **Purity Requirement** (纯度要求): Perfect (完美) (75-95%)

## Risks and Safeguards (风险与保障)

- **Breath Disruption** (呼吸紊乱): Loss of control over personal breathing patterns (失去对个人呼吸模式的控制)
- **Pressure Damage** (压力损伤): Creating unsafe pressure differentials affecting ears, sinuses, or lungs (产生影响耳朵、鼻窦或肺部的不安全压差)
- **Oxygen Deprivation** (缺氧): Accidentally removing breathable air from the caster's vicinity (意外地从施法者附近移除可呼吸的空气)
- **Weather Instability** (天气不稳定): Unintended atmospheric effects spreading beyond the intended area (意外的大气影响蔓延到预定区域之外)
- **Dispersal Effect** (扩散效应): Caster's consciousness becoming too diffuse during advanced techniques (施法者的意识在高级技巧中变得过于分散)
