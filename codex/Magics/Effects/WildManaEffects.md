## Understanding Wild Mana (野生マナ) Contamination (汚染)

Wild Mana (野生マナ) contamination (汚染) occurs when a spellcaster or environment is exposed to excessive amounts of raw, unpurified magical energy. While all magic users naturally process small amounts of Wild Mana (野生マナ), exposure beyond an individual's filtration capacity can lead to a spectrum of magical maladies.

The severity of effects depends on several factors:
- Concentration of Wild Mana (野生マナの濃度)
- Duration of exposure (曝露期間)
- Individual's Mana Capacity (個人のマナ容量)
- Previous exposure history (以前の曝露履歴)
- Protective measures employed (講じられた保護対策)
