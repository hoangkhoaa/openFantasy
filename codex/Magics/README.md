## Magical Elements (魔法元素)

The OpenFantasy world recognizes eight primary elements arranged in complementary pairs: (OpenFantasy 世界承认八种主要元素，它们以互补的成对方式排列：)

| Element (元素) | Opposed Element (对立元素) | Associated Mana (相关魔力) |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Fire (火) | Water (水) | Pyromana (火焰魔力) |
| Water (水) | Fire (火) | Hydromana (水魔力) |
| Earth (土) | Air (气) | Terramana (土魔力) |
| Air (气) | Earth (土) | Aeromana (气魔力) |
| Light (光) | Shadow (影) | Luxmana (光魔力) |
| Shadow (影) | Light (光) | Umbramana (影魔力) |
| Life (生) | Death (死) | Vitaemana (生命魔力) |
| Death (死) | Life (生) | Mortismana (死亡魔力) |

Base Mana serves as the foundation for all magical workings, with elemental mana types adding specific properties. (基本魔力是所有魔法运作的基础，元素魔力类型添加了特定的属性。)

## Enhancement and Progression (增强与进阶)

Character advancement in magical ability follows several paths: (角色在魔法能力方面的进步遵循以下几种途径：)

1. **Capacity Development**: Increasing the amount of mana a character can hold (容量发展：增加角色可以持有的魔力数量)
2. **Purification Skill**: Improving the quality of mana a character can produce (净化技能：提高角色可以产生的魔力质量)
3. **Pattern Complexity**: Learning more intricate spell structures (模式复杂性：学习更复杂的法术结构)
4. **Elemental Attunement**: Developing affinity with specific elements (元素协调：发展与特定元素的亲和力)
5. **Specialization**: Focusing on particular magical disciplines (专业化：专注于特定的魔法学科)

Enhancement buffs can temporarily boost these abilities beyond natural limits. (强化增益可以暂时将这些能力提升到超出自然极限。)
