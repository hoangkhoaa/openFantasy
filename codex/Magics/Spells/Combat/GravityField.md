## Core Principles (核心原理)

Gravity Field (重力场) manipulates the fundamental force of gravity within a defined area. (操纵在限定区域内的基本重力。) Unlike simpler directional force spells, Gravity Field (重力场) creates a persistent area where gravity's strength and direction can be altered. (不像更简单的定向力法术，重力场创造了一个持续的区域，在该区域内重力的强度和方向可以被改变。) This makes it particularly useful for area control, mobility enhancement, and environmental manipulation. (这使得它特别适用于区域控制、移动增强和环境操纵。)

3. **Directional Matrix (方向矩阵)**: The optional reorientation of gravitational pull (重力牵引的可选重新定向)
4. **Stability Framework (稳定性框架)**: The pattern elements maintaining field coherence (维持场连贯性的模式元素)
5. **Selectivity Filter (选择性过滤器)**: The optional component allowing selective application to specific targets (允许选择性应用于特定目标的可选组件)

What distinguishes Gravity Field (重力场) from many other combat spells is its reliance on technical execution rather than mana purity. (与其他许多战斗法术不同的是，重力场依赖于技术执行而不是魔法纯度。) While quality mana certainly helps, masters of this discipline focus primarily on precise pattern manipulation and field stability techniques.
