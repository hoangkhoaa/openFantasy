## Historical Note (历史记录)

Dancing Ember was formalized as a teaching spell by the renowned pyromancer Ignis Lumina (伊格尼斯·卢米娜), who recognized that many apprentices were injuring themselves attempting more complex fire magic without proper foundation. She designed this spell specifically to teach control before power, a philosophy summarized in her famous quote: "Learn to dance with a single ember before you attempt to conduct a symphony of flames."

舞动的余烬是由著名的火焰术士 Ignis Lumina (伊格尼斯·卢米娜) 正式确立为教学法术的，她意识到许多学徒在没有适当的基础的情况下尝试更复杂的火焰魔法时会受伤。她专门设计了这个法术来教导控制先于力量，这种哲学概括在她的名言中：“在你尝试指挥火焰交响乐之前，先学会与单个余烬共舞。”
