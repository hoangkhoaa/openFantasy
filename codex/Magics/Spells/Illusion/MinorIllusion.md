# **Minor Illusion (次级幻象)**

> *"Reality is but a canvas awaiting the illusion artist's touch."*  
> — Mirala Shadowweaver (米拉拉·影织者), Illusion Grandmaster (幻术大师)

- **Difficulty (难度)**: Novice (新手)

- **Range (范围)**: Within 15 feet (15 英尺内)
- **Components (成分)**: Somatic (姿势), Verbal (言语)

## Mana Mechanics (法力机制)

### Base Mana Function (基础法力功能)
The Base Mana (基础法力) component (60%) provides:
- Structural framework for the illusion pattern (幻象模式的结构框架)
- Stability and duration maintenance (稳定性和持续时间维护)
- Connection to the caster's concentration (与施法者专注的连接)
- Boundary definition and spatial anchoring (边界定义和空间锚定)

### Umbramana Function (幽影法力功能)
The Shadow mana (阴影法力) component (30%) contributes:
- Visual/sensory obscuration capabilities (视觉/感官模糊能力)
- Reality distortion properties (现实扭曲属性)
- Light/perception manipulation (光线/感知操纵)
- Concealment of the magical nature of the effect (掩盖效果的魔法本质)

### Luxmana Function (辉光法力功能)
The Light mana (光法力) component (10%) provides:
- Visual clarity and detail enhancement (视觉清晰度和细节增强)
- Sensory precision and definition (感官精确度和定义)
- Reality mimicry qualities (现实模仿品质)
- Observer connection hooks (观察者连接钩)

### Specialization Process (专业化过程)
The caster must successfully:
1. Draw Base Mana (基础法力) from internal reserves (从内部储备中提取基础法力)
2. Transform approximately 40% of this Base Mana (基础法力) into specialized forms (将大约 40% 的基础法力转化为专业形式)
3. Maintain the correct proportional balance (60:30:10) (保持正确的比例平衡 (60:30:10))
4. Establish the harmonics between opposing elements (Umbramana (幽影法力) and Luxmana (辉光法力)) (在对立元素之间建立谐波（幽影法力和辉光法力）)

## Practical Effect (实际效果)

Minor Illusion (次级幻象) creates a single sensory illusion within range:
- A visual illusion no larger than a 5-foot cube (不大于 5 英尺立方体的视觉幻象)
- A sound emanating from a point in range (whisper to conversation volume) (从范围内的某个点发出的声音（耳语到对话音量）)
- A simple scent detectable within 10 feet (可在 10 英尺范围内检测到的简单气味)
- A minor tactile sensation (temperature change, light breeze, gentle touch) (轻微的触觉（温度变化、微风、轻柔的触摸）)

The spell can create only one type of sensory effect at a time. (该法术一次只能产生一种类型的感官效果。) The illusion has no physical substance and cannot cause damage, create light that illuminates areas, or produce sound loud enough to cause damage or be heard more than 100 feet away. (幻象没有物理实体，不能造成伤害，不能产生照亮区域的光，也不能产生足够大的声音造成伤害或被超过 100 英尺的距离听到。)
