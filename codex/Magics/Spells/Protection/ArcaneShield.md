## Pattern Structure

The Arcane Shield pattern consists of five primary elements:

2. **Energy Matrix (能量矩阵)**: Distributes magical force evenly throughout the shield (在整个护盾中均匀地分配魔法力量)
3. **Deflection Array (偏转阵列)**: Angles the shield's surface to divert incoming force (倾斜护盾表面以转移进入的力量)
4. **Filtration Web (过滤网)**: Selectively allows certain energies to pass through (有选择地允许某些能量通过)
5. **Anchor Points (锚点)**: Connects the shield to the caster's personal energy (将护盾连接到施法者的个人能量)

These elements form a spherical pattern that surrounds the caster in a protective bubble.
