## Overview (概述)

Mana mechanics (法力机制) 规定了在 OpenFantasy 中如何收集、存储、提炼和利用魔法能量。本文档概述了法力操纵不同方面之间的基本关系。

## Two-Phase Mana Conversion Process (两相法力转换过程)

所有法术施放的基础在于一个两相转换过程，该过程将环境中的 Wild Mana (狂野法力) 转化为可用的魔法能量：

### Phase 1 (阶段 1): Wild Mana (狂野法力) to Base Mana (基础法力) (Effectiveness-Focused (以效率为中心))
- **Definition (定义)**: The biological process of absorbing Wild Mana (狂野法力) from the environment and converting it to stable Base Mana (基础法力) within the caster's body (从环境中吸收 Wild Mana (狂野法力) 并将其转换为施法者体内稳定的 Base Mana (基础法力) 的生物学过程)
- **Key Metric (关键指标)**: Conversion Effectiveness (转换效率) (how efficiently Wild Mana (狂野法力) becomes Base Mana (基础法力)) (Wild Mana (狂野法力) 转化为 Base Mana (基础法力) 的效率)
- **Primary Bottleneck (主要瓶颈)**: Mana Capacity (法力容量) (internal storage limitation) (内部存储限制)
- **Primary Function (主要功能)**: Creates the caster's internal magical reserves (创造施法者的内部魔法储备)

> **Important Note (重要提示)**: All mana stored within living creatures is Base Mana (基础法力). Wild Mana (狂野法力) cannot be stored directly in biological systems without causing harm. (所有储存在生物体内的法力都是 Base Mana (基础法力)。 Wild Mana (狂野法力) 不能直接储存在生物系统中，否则会造成伤害。)

### Phase 2 (阶段 2): Base Mana (基础法力) to Elemental Mana (元素法力) (Purity-Focused (以纯度为中心))
- **Definition (定义)**: The conscious, technical process of refining Base Mana (基础法力) into specialized Elemental Mana (元素法力) for spellcasting (有意识地将 Base Mana (基础法力) 提炼成专门用于法术施放的 Elemental Mana (元素法力) 的技术过程)
- **Key Metric (关键指标)**: Purity Percentage (纯度百分比) (ratio of refined Elemental Mana (元素法力) to total energies used) (提炼后的 Elemental Mana (元素法力) 与所用总能量的比率)
- **Primary Bottleneck (主要瓶颈)**: Skill and technique (training-dependent) (技能和技巧（取决于训练）)
- **Primary Function (主要功能)**: Creates specialized magical energy suitable for spellcasting (创造适合法术施放的专业魔法能量)

These two phases operate in sequence but require different skills and aptitudes, explaining why some casters may have large reserves but poor control, or vice versa. (这两个阶段按顺序运行，但需要不同的技能和能力，这解释了为什么一些施法者可能拥有大量的储备但控制力差，反之亦然。)

## Mana Purity and Spell Effectiveness (法力纯度与法术效果)

Mana Purity (法力纯度) represents the quality of magical energy after Phase 2 conversion. It indicates how much of the energy has been successfully refined from Base Mana (基础法力) into the specific Elemental Mana (元素法力) required for a spell. (表示阶段 2 转换后魔法能量的质量。它表明有多少能量已成功地从 Base Mana (基础法力) 提炼成法术所需的特定 Elemental Mana (元素法力)。)

### Purity as a Percentage (纯度百分比)

Purity (纯度) is calculated using the formula: (使用以下公式计算：)
```
Purity % = (Refined Elemental Mana (提炼后的元素法力)) / (Refined Elemental Mana (提炼后的元素法力) + Residual Wild Mana (残留的狂野法力)) × 100%
```

Practitioners exhibit natural variations in their aptitude for the two conversion phases: (从业者在两个转换阶段的能力方面表现出自然差异：)

- **Adaptive Caster (自适应施法者)**: Ability to modify approach based on magical conditions (能够根据魔法条件修改方法)

These natural tendencies are typically apparent by adolescence and influence recommended specialization paths. (这些自然倾向通常在青春期就显现出来，并影响推荐的专业化道路。)
