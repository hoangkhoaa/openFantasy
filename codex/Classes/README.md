## 元素构成

每个职业都具有塑造其能力的特征元素构成：

| Class (职业) | Exanthis (能量) | Soul (灵魂) | Mana Capacity (魔法容量) | Primary Magical Affinity (主要魔法亲和性) |
|-------|----------|------|---------------|--------------------------|
| **Warrior (战士)** | 50-70% | 20-35% | 10-20% | Terramana (岩石能量) (Stability (稳定)), Base Mana (基础能量) (Force (力量)) |
| **Mage (法师)** | 10-25% | 15-35% | 50-70% | Varies by specialization (因专业而异) |
| **Rogue (盗贼)** | 30-45% | 40-60% | 10-25% | Umbramana (阴影能量) (Concealment (隐藏)), Aeromana (空气能量) (Mobility (移动性)) |
| **Cleric (牧师)** | 20-35% | 30-50% | 25-45% | Luxmana (光能量) (Light (光)), Vitamana (生命能量) (Healing (治疗)) |
| **Ranger (游侠)** | 35-55% | 30-45% | 15-30% | Vitamana (生命能量) (Nature (自然)), Aeromana (空气能量) (Movement (运动)) |
| **Artificer (工匠)** | 25-40% | 20-35% | 35-55% | Crystallmana (水晶能量) (Structure (结构)), Terramana (岩石能量) (Solidity (坚固)) |
| **Bard (吟游诗人)** | 15-30% | 35-55% | 25-45% | Fulgumana (声音能量) (Sound (声音)), Aeromana (空气能量) (Communication (沟通)) |
| **Druid (德鲁伊)** | 20-35% | 30-45% | 30-50% | Vitamana (生命能量) (Growth (生长)), Terramana (岩石能量) (Earth (大地)) |
| **Monk (武僧)** | 40-55% | 30-50% | 10-25% | Base Mana (基础能量) (Internal Energy (内在能量)), Aeromana (空气能量) (Movement (运动)) |
| **Warlock (术士)** | 15-30% | 25-40% | 40-60% | Aetheramana (虚空能量) (Void (虚空)), Varies by pact (因契约而异) |

这种元素平衡直接影响一个职业对某些特殊魔法形式的天然亲和力及其整体魔法能力：

- **Specialization Efficiency (专业效率)**: Classes with higher Mana Capacity typically achieve better elemental specialization rates (魔法容量较高的职业通常获得更好的元素专业化率)
- **Mana Purification (魔法净化)**: The ratio of Soul to Mana Capacity affects a class's ability to purify Wild Mana into Base Mana (灵魂与魔法容量的比率影响一个职业将狂野魔法净化为基础魔法的能力)
- **Magical Stamina (魔法耐力)**: Higher Exanthis provides better physical endurance but typically reduces magical efficiency (更高的能量提供更好的身体耐力，但通常会降低魔法效率)
- **Hybrid Potential (混合潜力)**: Classes with more balanced compositions often excel at combining different elemental types (具有更平衡构成的职业通常擅长结合不同的元素类型)
