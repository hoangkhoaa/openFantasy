## Overview (概述)

Mana Wisps (法力精灵) are rare, ephemeral beings that exist as direct manifestations of nearly pure Base Mana (基础法力) that has achieved consciousness. 法力精灵是稀有的、短暂的生物，它们是几乎纯净的、已经获得意识的基础法力的直接体现。 Unlike most magical creatures, Wisps are not born through biological processes but form spontaneously in areas with exceptionally high magical saturation, particularly near ley line (能量线) convergences, ancient ritual sites, or in the aftermath of powerful magical events. 与大多数魔法生物不同，精灵不是通过生物过程诞生的，而是在魔法饱和度极高的区域自发形成的，特别是在能量线汇聚处、古代仪式场所或强大的魔法事件之后。 They represent the threshold where magical energy gains sufficient complexity to develop self-awareness, existing as living embodiments of the fundamental magical principles that govern OpenFantasy. 它们代表了魔法能量获得足够的复杂性以发展自我意识的阈值，作为管理OpenFantasy的基本魔法原则的活生生的化身而存在。
