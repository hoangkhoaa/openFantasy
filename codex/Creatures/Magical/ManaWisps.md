## Overview (概要)

**Mana Wisps (マナ・ウィスプ)** are rare, ephemeral beings that exist as direct manifestations of nearly pure Base Mana (基本マナ) that has achieved consciousness. Unlike most magical creatures, Wisps are not born through biological processes but form spontaneously in areas with exceptionally high magical saturation, particularly near ley line (レイライン) convergences, ancient ritual sites, or in the aftermath of powerful magical events. They represent the threshold where magical energy gains sufficient complexity to develop self-awareness, existing as living embodiments of the fundamental magical principles that govern OpenFantasy.
