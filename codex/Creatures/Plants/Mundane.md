# OpenFantasy: Magic System

This document outlines the magic system used in OpenFantasy. The system is designed to be flexible and modular, allowing Game Masters (게임 마스터) to customize it to their specific needs.

## Core Concepts

*   **Mana (마나):** The source of all magical power. Measured in Mana Points (마나 포인트).
*   **Spells (주문):** Specific magical effects created by manipulating Mana (마나).
*   **Schools of Magic (마법 학파):** Broad categories of magic, such as Evocation (소환 마법) or Necromancy (강령술).
*   **Casting Time (시전 시간):** The time required to cast a Spell (주문).
*   **Components (구성 요소):** Materials or actions required to cast a Spell (주문). These can be Verbal (언어적), Somatic (신체적), or Material (물질적).

## Schools of Magic (마법 학파)

The following table lists the available Schools of Magic (마법 학파) in OpenFantasy:

| School (학파) | Description (설명) |
|---|---|
| Abjuration (방어 마법) | Spells (주문) that protect against harm or negate magical effects. |
| Conjuration (소환 마법) | Spells (주문) that summon creatures or objects. |
| Divination (점술) | Spells (주문) that reveal information or predict the future. |
| Enchantment (강화 마법) | Spells (주문) that alter the properties of objects or creatures. |
| Evocation (소환 마법) | Spells (주문) that create destructive or transformative effects. |
| Illusion (환영 마법) | Spells (주문) that create illusions or deceive the senses. |
| Necromancy (강령술) | Spells (주문) that manipulate life and death. |
| Transmutation (변환 마법) | Spells (주문) that alter the physical form of objects or creatures. |

## Spellcasting Mechanics

1.  **Choose a Spell (주문 선택):** The caster selects a Spell (주문) they know.
2.  **Pay the Mana Cost (마나 소모):** The caster spends the required Mana Points (마나 포인트).
3.  **Provide Components (구성 요소 제공):** The caster provides any required Verbal (언어적), Somatic (신체적), or Material (물질적) Components (구성 요소).
4.  **Cast the Spell (주문 시전):** The caster completes the Casting Time (시전 시간).
5.  **Resolve the Spell Effect (주문 효과 해결):** The Spell (주문) takes effect.

## Example Spell (주문 예시): Fireball (화염구)

*   **School (학파):** Evocation (소환 마법)
*   **Mana Cost (마나 소모):** 5
*   **Casting Time (시전 시간):** 1 action (1 액션)
*   **Components (구성 요소):** Verbal (언어적), Somatic (신체적)
*   **Effect (효과):** Deals 8d6 fire damage in a 20-foot radius. (반경 20피트 내에 8d6의 화염 피해를 입힙니다.)

## Customization

Game Masters (게임 마스터) can customize the magic system by:

*   Adding new Schools of Magic (마법 학파).
*   Creating new Spells (주문).
*   Adjusting the Mana Cost (마나 소모) and Casting Time (시전 시간) of existing Spells (주문).
*   Introducing new Components (구성 요소).
